Privacy Policy

At AI Job Board, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our platform.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal details (name, email, contact number, address, date of birth)
  • Employment-related information (resume, skills, experience, job preferences)
  • Usage data (IP address, device info, browsing activities on our site)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To connect job seekers with potential employers
  • To provide AI-powered job recommendations and resume scoring
  • To send important notifications, job alerts, and updates
  • For internal analytics to improve our services

3. Data Sharing and Security

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. Your data is shared only with authorized employers based on your application and account settings. We use industry-standard encryption and secure servers to protect your information.

4. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to enhance user experience and track site performance. You can manage or disable cookies from your browser settings.

5. Your Rights

  • Access and update your personal information at any time
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Control visibility of your resume and contact details

6. Updates to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be notified on this page with an updated "Last Modified" date.
